work

  • 2012.06 - present
    Professor
    UNSW Sydney
    I decided to move to UNSW Sydney on a Vice-Chancellor's postdoctoral fellowship in 2012, mentored by Toby Walsh. Before I could take up the postdoctoral fellowship, I was awarded a DECRA (Discovery Early Career Award) by the Australian Research Council (ARC), which was followed up by a Future Fellowship. After 3 successful rounds of promotion, I became a professor in 2022. Since 2018 I have also been the Associate Head of School (Research).
